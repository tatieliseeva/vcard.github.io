Simulations

Non-linear processes are very difficult to tackle analytically and quickly tend to be overwhelming when trying to understand them. That tasks can appear in structural analysis, heat transfer or mass transport, nuclear medicine. Profound knowledge regarding statistical process simulations (for example with Monte-Carlo methods) or finite element methods (FEM) allow us to take on such challenges as well.
Having parameters of your task, we can estimate how the whole system is going to behave or restore the important missing information.
